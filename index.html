About TriBite

— CALCULATE. PLAY. WRITE. —

TriBite is a small personal project: a calculator, a pixel-art endless runner, and a notes app, all living in one place. It started as a way to build something genuinely useful and a little fun, rather than three separate throwaway tools.

What's inside

Calculate It. — a full expression calculator with trigonometry, logarithms, factorials, parentheses, and a polynomial solver that shows its work.

Play. — a pixelated endless runner. Dodge crates, collect coins, and watch for the turns.

Take Notes. — rich text notes with drawing, image attachments, and PDF export, all saved right on your own device.

Who's behind it

TriBite is made and maintained by HIMworks — a one-person project, built and improved a little at a time.

How your data works

Everything you create inside the app — notes, drawings, game progress — stays stored locally in your own browser. Nothing you write is sent to a server or seen by anyone else. See the Privacy Policy for the full details.

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information TriBite and its landing site collect, and how it's used. TriBite is a personal project — this policy is written plainly, not by a legal team, but it aims to be accurate and complete.

What TriBite itself stores

Notes, drawings, account names, PINs, and game progress you create inside the TriBite app are saved using your browser's local storage, directly on your own device. This data is never transmitted to, or stored on, any server we control. Clearing your browser's site data, or using a different browser/device, will remove or not show this information.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand overall visits — such as how many people use the site, roughly which pages are visited, and general device/location information. This data is aggregated and used only to understand traffic; it is not linked to anything you write inside the app itself.
